Currently, I am a postdoc researcher, working with Anton Frisk Kockum, in the Applied Quantum Physics Laboratory division, in the Department of Microtechnology and Nanoscience at Chalmers University of Technology, in Gothenburg, Sweden.

I did my Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Kavita Dorai and Prof. Arvind at the IISER Mohali , India. My thesis dissertation, titled Novel techniques for efficient quantum state tomography and quantum process tomography and their experimental implementation, focused on advancing methods in quantum state and process tomography.

My research interests are:

  • Quantum state tomography

  • Quantum process tomography

  • Quantum optimal control

  • Quantum computing with superconducting qubits and nuclear spin qubits

  • Linear algebra

  • NMR spectroscopy


If you are an experimentalist in quantum computing and are interested in performing tomography for your systems (discrete or continuous), I would be happy to discuss potential collaborations. Theorists are also welcome to reach out for collaborative opportunities.
